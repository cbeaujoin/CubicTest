About CubicTest

CubicTest has been in the works since spring 2005 and has been actively developed since then. CubicTest started as a research project at Bekk Consulting AS to make web acceptance testing more available to all stakeholders of a project. CubicTest is now in a stable and complete release, fit for use in projects. Four master's theses have contributed to CubicTest, as well as continuous work by the project lead.
